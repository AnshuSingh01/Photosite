About Photonics

The IEEE Photonics Society is dedicated to advancing the scientific and educational aspects of photonics. The Society strives for the advancement of the theory and practice of photonics, optoelectronics, and electro-optics engineering and science, and of the allied arts and sciences, and for the maintenance of high professional standards among its members, all in consonance with the Constitution and Bylaws of the IEEE.

Our Mission is to foster the development and facilitate the exchange of scientific and technological knowledge in photonics that benefits members, the profession and humanity.

Our Vision is to be the most recognized and respected global organization in photonics and optoelectronics.

IEEE Photonics Society: Executive Committee (2024-2025)

The Photonics Society strives to advance innovation, education, and fundamental and applied research in photonics. Photonics focuses on the generation, transmission, modulation, signal processing, amplification, detection and sensing of light. Photonics research emphasizes applications in communications, imaging, sensing, displays, and energy conversion. The field encompasses both theoretical frameworks and practical applications that have revolutionized modern technology.

The Society provides aid in promoting close cooperation and exchange of technical information among its Members and Affiliates, and to this end holds meetings for the presentation of papers and their discussion, sponsors appropriate periodicals and special technical publications, and through its committees studies and provides for the needs of its members and affiliates.
